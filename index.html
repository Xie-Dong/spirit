Spirit Biobank

Demo »

Concept

Spirit is a biobanking application, developed inhouse for Actelion Pharmaceuticals, Switzerland. The primary objective was to help scientists/technicians doing their studies. It covers all aspects from study design to biosample management, location handling and result analysis.
